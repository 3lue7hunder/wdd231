Trail Explorer - Site Plan

Site Name

Trail Explorer - This site is designed to help hikers discover and explore local hiking trails with relevant details like difficulty, length, elevation, and scenic features.

Site Purpose

The site provides information about local hiking trails, including trail details, user reviews, and weather updates for safety.

Scenarios

  • What are the best hiking trails near me based on difficulty level?
  • How can I leave a review about a trail I recently visited?

Color Schema

  • Green (#4CAF50) - Used for headings and accents.
  • Beige (#F5F5DC) - Used as the background color.
  • Dark Gray (#333333) - Used for body text.

Typography

Fonts used:

  • Montserrat - Used for headings and titles.
  • Roboto - Used for body text.
